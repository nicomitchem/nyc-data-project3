Graffiti in Brooklyn has been heavily influenced by the borough's history of street art and activism. In the 1970s and 1980s, writers like DONDI and SEEN gained fame for their colorful murals and intricate designs. More recently, Brooklyn has become a hub of street art and graffiti-inspired installations, with events like the Bushwick Collective's annual mural festival drawing thousands of visitors each year.
